Hotspots

The hotspots are derived from satellite data and may be affected by cloud cover and the satellite coverage

A low pressure system developing over the South China Sea brought scattered showers to the Philippines and some parts of the Mekong sub-region. There were also scattered showers over the southern ASEAN region, but isolated hotspots with localised smoke haze continued to be observed in South Sumatra, Lampung and Central Kalimantan. Over the next few days, more showers can be expected over the Philippines, and parts of the Mekong sub-region. The prevailing winds are forecast to blow from the northeast or east. In the southern ASEAN region, rainy conditions are forecast over areas along the equator, but drier weather is expected to return to the southern parts of Kalimantan and Sumatra, and Java. The prevailing winds are expected to blow mainly from the southeast or southwest.

Updated 5:32 PM 4 Nov
